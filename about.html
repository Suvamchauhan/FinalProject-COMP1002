Instructions for the Game:

Step:1

    First, the player has to choose any number from 1 to 10.

Step:2

    Player has to enter the number they choose to be correct inside a insert box.

Step:3

    In order to choose the number ,players can also use the upward and backward clicking option on the insert box.

step:4

    After the number is being added , the player needs to press the Guess button to see if the chosen number is correct or not.

step:5

If the number is correct then the player wins, else the guess is incorrect, and the player can continue to guess the number until they have 4 tries left.

step:6

    The Tries box represents the number of tries made to guess the number. The player can try to guess the number until 4 tries and if player could not guess the number until 4 tries then the player loses the game.

step:7

    Either if the guess is correct or the player is out of guess then game will restart again.

Hope you will enjoy the game !
